Pic Merger - Support Page

App Introduction

Pic Merger is a powerful image stitching tool that supports multiple collage methods, including 4-grid, 9-grid, horizontal stitching, and vertical stitching.

Main features:

  • Multiple collage layouts: 4-grid, 9-grid, horizontal stitching, vertical stitching
  • Support for drag-and-drop to adjust image order
  • Adjustable image spacing
  • HD export
  • Pro version with no ads and no image count limit

User Guide

1. Select Images

After opening the app, tap the "Select Images" button to choose the images you want to stitch from your photo library.

2. Adjust Image Order

After selecting images, you can adjust their order by dragging them.

3. Select Collage Method

Select the desired collage method at the bottom: 4-grid, 9-grid, horizontal stitching, or vertical stitching.

4. Adjust Spacing

Use the slider to adjust the spacing between images for the best effect.

5. Save Image

Tap the "Save" button to save the stitched image to your photo library.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why can I only add 9 images?

A: Non-Pro users can add up to 9 images. Upgrade to Pro to add unlimited images.

Q: How to restore purchases?

A: Tap the "Restore Purchases" button, and the system will automatically restore your previous purchase records.

Q: What is the resolution of saved images?

A: Pro users get higher resolution images, while non-Pro users get slightly lower resolution.

Q: Which devices are supported?

A: The app supports iPhone and iPad devices with iOS 15.0 or later.
