Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

Puzzle Master is a local-only photo collage tool. We do not collect, store, or upload any of your personal information.

When you use the following features:

  • Select Photos: Only selects photos from your device's photo library for collage purposes. We do not access or transmit your photo data.
  • Save Photos: Only saves the merged collage photo to your photo library.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no information usage issues. All photo processing is performed locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

We do not transmit any data to servers. All photos you select and the merged collages are stored only locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Policy Updates

If we need to update this privacy policy, we will notify you through in-app announcements.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the "Contact Us" feature in the app.
